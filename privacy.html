Privacy Policy

How We Handle Your Trust

Last Updated: March 2026

The Collective respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide when interacting with our website, application forms, and community platforms.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you apply to join The Collective ecosystem and the 45-Day Paving Period.

01 — Information We Collect

What We Know About You

When you interact with The Collective, we may collect the following types of information.

Personal Information

Information you voluntarily provide when applying or joining the waitlist, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Professional background
  • Business or organization information
  • Responses to application or screening questions
Technical Information

When visiting our website or landing pages, we may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • IP address
  • Website usage data

This helps us improve the performance and functionality of our digital platforms.

02 — How We Use Your Information

Purpose of Collection

The information you provide may be used for the following purposes.

Application and Screening

To evaluate eligibility and determine alignment with The Collective's mission and standards.

Communication

To send:

  • Onboarding instructions
  • Program updates
  • Important announcements related to the 45-Day Paving Period
  • Community invitations and notifications
Community Management

To manage participation within:

  • The Collective ecosystem
  • Online discussion groups
  • Community platforms such as the official Facebook group
Program Improvement

To analyze participation and improve the structure, experience, and impact of The Collective programs.

03 — Community Platforms

Third-Party Communities

Participants may be invited to join community platforms such as The Collective Facebook Community.

These platforms are governed by the privacy policies of their respective providers. Any information you share within these platforms may be visible to other members of the community.

Participants are encouraged to avoid sharing confidential, proprietary, or sensitive information within public discussions.

04 — Email Communications

Staying in Touch

By submitting an application or joining the waitlist, you consent to receive communications from The Collective related to:

  • Application updates
  • Program onboarding
  • Community announcements
  • Educational or mission-related updates

You may unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time through the unsubscribe link included in email messages.

05 — Data Protection

Keeping Your Data Safe

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no digital platform or transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

06 — Sharing of Information

Who Else Sees Your Data

The Collective does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers that support the operation of our programs, such as:

  • Email communication platforms
  • Website or funnel hosting services
  • Community management tools

These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their services and are expected to maintain confidentiality.

07 — Intellectual Contributions

Your Ideas & Insights

Participants may share insights, reflections, and leadership ideas within the program. Participants retain ownership of their ideas. However, by participating in The Collective ecosystem, you acknowledge that selected insights or contributions may be referenced within:

  • Community documentation
  • Educational materials
  • The Collective Podcast: Archive of Thoughts

Participation in media features will only occur with consent.

08 — Data Retention

How Long We Keep It

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Manage applications and program participation
  • Maintain community records
  • Improve The Collective ecosystem

You may request removal of your information from our records at any time by contacting us.

09 — Third-Party Links

External Platforms

Our website or materials may contain links to third-party platforms such as social media communities. The Collective is not responsible for the privacy practices or policies of those external platforms.

10 — Changes to This Policy

Keeping Things Current

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements in our systems, services, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted with a revised Last Updated date.

11 — Contact Information

Get in Touch

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, you may contact us through the official communication channels of The Collective.

Contact The Collective

For privacy-related questions or data removal requests, please reach out through our official communication channels or via the contact form on our homepage. Please reference "Privacy Request" in your message.
